Shri Anubhav Mohanty is loved by people of all age groups and all sections. Yet it’s the youth who follows Shri Anubhav Mohanty the most. His humble nature, easy accessibility, love for all and passion for fitness is the reason why he is so endeared by the Youth Of Odisha. Shri Anubhav Mohanty is not like any other run of the mill fitness icons. At the very young age of 17 years only, Shri Anubhav Mohanty was diagnosed with diabetes & since then he has been administering himself with insulin shots daily. Yet he has never let his condition come in his way of fitness. Shri Anubhav Mohanty has inspired lakhs of youths towards living a fit and healthy life even being diabetic. Due to his macho and manly image, young fans of Shri Mohanty see him as their big brother and gave him the title of “Bhaijaan”. Hence, to honour & reciprocate the youths unconditional love towards him, Shri Mohanty has started his very own fitness website where his fans/followers can very easily connect with him, get physically trained, medically monitored, tracked, guided, adviced by certified professionals & make themselves examples/icons for all others. Shri Mohanty has also started his own Gym Equipments, Outfits, Accessories, E-Cycles, Nutritions etc.. For more details, you can please login to www.workoutbox.in & stay updated about Shri Anubhav Mohanty’s fitness secrets.




Shri Anubhav Mohanty joined the Biju Janata Dal led by Hon’ble Shri Naveen Patnaik ji on the 27th of April 2013. He was elected unopposed to the Rajya Sabha on 12th June 2014 and was the Youngest Member of Rajya Sabha. On 23 of May 2019, he was elected to the 17th Lok Sabha from Kendrapara Parliamentary Constituency of Odisha by a record winning-margin of more than 1.5 lakh votes.
