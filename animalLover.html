Shri Anubhav Mohanty is the reigning biggest superstar and highest paid actor in Odia Cinema ever. At a very young age of 17 or 18, Shri Anubhav Mohanty started his modelling career and in a very short time he won many big titles as a model. He was first featured in music video’s 20 seconds teaser titled “MO SHARMILIII” under the banner of a legendary film maker, Late Amiya Patnaik. His entry into music video world was highly appreciated which gave him his first break as the male lead in a full music video opposite to Late Deepa Sahoo, fondly called as ‘Deepa Nani’ by Shri Mohanty. This music video was produced by very famous TP Music, directed by Late Saroj Sathpathy and the title was “MU JHIA TE MADE IN ODISHA”. This was a massive hit and made Shri Mohanty gain a lot of popularity. His maximum number of superhit music videos were directed by his most favourite Shri J. P. Mohanty ji. Soon Shri Anubhav Mohanty became the most loved household name of Odisha. Such exemplary popularity didn’t go miss by movie makers such as Late Basant Naik and Late Hara Patnaik respectively. He debuted in the movie titled “I Love You” in 2004 under the banner, Brajraj Movies Produced by Shri Sanjay Naik (Tutu). This movie then broke all the records of past 60 years of Odia cinema then and became the highest grossing movie ever in Odia Box Office and still continues in theaters of Odisha with same love and acceptance. Shri Mohanty is the only actor of Odia cinema who has witnessed the maximum number of 100/150 days movies, All Time Blockbusters and even endorsed national brands which gained him the highest level of popularity amongst Odia Cinema Lovers. The journey still continues……….




Shri Anubhav Mohanty joined the Biju Janata Dal led by Hon’ble Shri Naveen Patnaik ji on the 27th of April 2013. He was elected unopposed to the Rajya Sabha on 12th June 2014 and was the Youngest Member of Rajya Sabha. On 23 of May 2019, he was elected to the 17th Lok Sabha from Kendrapara Parliamentary Constituency of Odisha by a record winning-margin of more than 1.5 lakh votes.
