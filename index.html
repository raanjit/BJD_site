Shri Anubhav Mohanty

Shri Anubhav Mohanty joined the Biju Janata Dal led by Hon’ble Shri Naveen Patnaik ji on the 27th of April 2013. He was elected unopposed to the Rajya Sabha on 12th June 2014 and was the Youngest Member of Rajya Sabha. On 23 of May 2019, he was elected to the 17th Lok Sabha from Kendrapara Parliamentary Constituency of Odisha by a record winning-margin of more than 1.5 lakh votes. Shri Anubhav Mohanty is known as one of the most Youngest Politicians Of Odisha. He is still the leading & highest paid Odia Actor and reigns the hearts of every Odia with the highest fan following ever. He is popularly known and addressed as the "Ollywood Badshah", "King" and "Bhaijaan" lovingly by his fans..
