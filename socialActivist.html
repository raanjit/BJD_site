Shri Anubhav Mohanty was always inclined towards helping people who are in need as he himself is an emotional person. His life as a social activist started from his childhood itself. He would dedicate whatever time he would get out of his studies, career, family, profession, political responsibilities towards social work and attending to the needy. Shri Anubhav Mohanty has recently started a “Register as an Organ Donor” drive. He himself has pledged/registered to donate his organs. Shri Anubhav Mohanty has also worked extensively for treatment of CLEFT patients and for help of People with disabilities. He has a very special inclination towards helping, supporting & loving Senior Citizens who are ignored and abandoned by their own children for dirty or whatever reasons. Shri Mohanty is an animal lover too. Many examples are there which show how innocent, clean, clear & loveable his heart is.




Shri Anubhav Mohanty joined the Biju Janata Dal led by Hon’ble Shri Naveen Patnaik ji on the 27th of April 2013. He was elected unopposed to the Rajya Sabha on 12th June 2014 and was the Youngest Member of Rajya Sabha. On 23 of May 2019, he was elected to the 17th Lok Sabha from Kendrapara Parliamentary Constituency of Odisha by a record winning-margin of more than 1.5 lakh votes.
