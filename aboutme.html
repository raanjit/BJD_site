Mission & Vision

Shri Anubhav Mohanty has always been very much inspired by the ideologies of Late Biju Babu and Hon. CM Shri Naveen Patnaik ji. His mission of inclusive development for Odisha and the people of Odisha aligns with the aim of his beloved Party, The Biju Janata Dal. Providing a secular, corruption free, welfare state based on social justice and equality for all has been guiding principles of Hon. CM Shri Naveen Patnaik ji. Shri Anubhav Mohanty, walking on his steps, is following him on the same lines and dreams of the day when Odisha’s model of development will be an example world-wide. Shri Mohanty has been extremely influenced by one of the most sensible statements of Hon’ble Shri Naveen Patnaik ji which is, "YOU CAN'T LEAD THE PEOPLE UNLESS YOU LOVE THEM"..




Shri Anubhav Mohanty joined the Biju Janata Dal led by Hon’ble Shri Naveen Patnaik ji on the 27th of April 2013. He was elected unopposed to the Rajya Sabha on 12th June 2014 and was the Youngest Member of Rajya Sabha. On 23 of May 2019, he was elected to the 17th Lok Sabha from Kendrapara Parliamentary Constituency of Odisha by a record winning-margin of more than 1.5 lakh votes.
