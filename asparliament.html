Shri Anubhav Mohanty joined the Biju Janata Dal on the 27th Of April 2013 because of his immense love & respect towards Hon’ble Shri Naveen Patnaik’s clean governance & habit of zero tolerance against corruption. Due to Shri Anubhav Mohanty’s inclination, zeal towards social reforms, his philanthropic activities, dedication towards his Party & his Leader, Hon. CM Shri Naveen Patnaik Sir put his faith in him and sent him to Rajya Sabha from Odisha and Shri Mohanty became the Youngest Member Of Parliament in the Rajya Sabha in the year 2014. After seeing his tremendous performance in Rajya Sabha, Hon. CM Shri Naveen Patnaik Sir gave Shri Anubhav Mohanty, an opportunity to contest for Lok Sabha Elections from Kendrapara Parliamentary Constituency, Odisha (dearly known as Sarvashresttha Karmbhoomi of Late Biju Babu). Shri Anubhav Mohanty not only won from Kendrapara but he also got a victory margin of more than 1.5 Lakh votes. Today he is honoured as the Youth Icon for crores of Young Odias. His performance as a Parliamentarian in the Lok Sabha has won billions of hearts & the people of Odisha have started expecting more powerful representations from him for the overall development of Kendrapara, Odisha. But Shri Mohanty has always dedicated all his good works to his Leader, Political Mentor, Number 1 Administrator in India, Most Ideal Chief Minister in India Hon’ble Shri Naveen Patnaik ji. He believes on a very important teaching of Shri Naveen Patnaik ji to the youth of India which is, “You Can’t Lead People Unless You Love Them”.




Shri Anubhav Mohanty joined the Biju Janata Dal led by Hon’ble Shri Naveen Patnaik ji on the 27th of April 2013. He was elected unopposed to the Rajya Sabha on 12th June 2014 and was the Youngest Member of Rajya Sabha. On 23 of May 2019, he was elected to the 17th Lok Sabha from Kendrapara Parliamentary Constituency of Odisha by a record winning-margin of more than 1.5 lakh votes.
